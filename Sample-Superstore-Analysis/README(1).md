# 📊 Sample Superstore Sales Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data using the Sample Superstore dataset to identify sales trends, profitable products, customer behavior, and business opportunities.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Pivot Charts
* Dashboard Design

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Top Customers
* Top Products
* Monthly Sales Performance

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Binders, Paper, and Furnishings were among the most profitable products.
* November, December, and September generated the highest sales volume.
* Several states showed weak performance and require further business attention.
* Orders with no discounts generally achieved better profitability.
* A small group of customers generated a significant portion of total sales.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Reduce unnecessary discounts to improve profit margins.
* Increase marketing activities during peak sales months.
* Focus on expanding sales of highly profitable products.
* Investigate the causes of poor performance in low-performing states.
* Strengthen customer retention strategies for top customers.
